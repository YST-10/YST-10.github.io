1. REVIEW OF ELECTROMAGNETIC THEORY 电磁学理论综述(没啥用)

(1) Physical Principles of RFID Systems RFID系统的物理原理

  • The vast majority of RFID systems operate according to the principle of inductive coupling. 绝大多数RFID系统都根据电感耦合的原理运行。

  • Electromagnetic fields – radio waves in the classic sense – are used in RFID systems that operate at above 30MHz.

    电磁场 - 从经典意义上讲的无线电波 - 被用于在30MHz以上运行的RFID系统中。

  • Electric fields play a secondary role and are only exploited for capacitive data transmission in close-coupling systems. Therefore, this type of field will not be discussed further. 电场起到次要作用，并且仅在近距离耦合系统中用于电容式数据传输。因此，这种类型的场不会再进一步讨论。

(2) Theory of Electromagnetism and Maxwell’s Equation 电磁学理论与麦克斯韦方程式

<1> 简介

  • James Clerk Maxwell (1831-1879) was a Scottish physicist and mathematician whose major discovery of the ether described the vast sea of space that made possible the transmission of light, heat and radio waves. 詹姆斯·克拉克·麦克斯韦（1831-1879年）是一位苏格兰物理学家和数学家，他的主要发现乙太描述了广阔的空间海洋，使光、热和无线电波的传输成为可能。

  • His extension of the electromagnetic theory of light led directly to Heinrich Hertz’s discovery of radio waves and to the related advances in science and technology of today. 他对光的电磁理论的扩展直接导致了海因里希·赫兹对无线电波的发现，以及今天科学技术的相关进展。

  • Maxwell’s book, The Complete Laws of Electrodynamics, defines the relationship between the electric field quantities and the magnetic field quantities. 麦克斯韦的著作《电动力学的完整法则》定义了电场量和磁场量之间的关系。

  • Maxwell was the first to correctly assemble the complete laws of electrodynamics in his classic text in 1873.

    麦克斯韦是第一个在他于1873年的经典著作中正确地整合电动力学的完整法则。

  • Modern electromagnetism theory is based on the four fundamental equations known as Maxwell’s equations.

    现代电磁理论基于被称为麦克斯韦方程的四个基本方程。

<2> Before Maxwell

  • Before Maxwell, the laws of electrodynamics, including Gauss’s law, Ampere’s law of magnetostatics, and Faraday’s law, were laws of electrostatics and did not predict waves. 在麦克斯韦之前，包括高斯定律、安培定律和法拉第定律在内的电动力学定律是静电学的定律，并且没有预测波动。

  • These laws correctly described what is known as the near field (i.e., the electrostatic field of an electric charge and the magnetostatic field of a current loop). 这些定律正确描述了所谓的近场（即电荷的静电场和电流环的磁静场）。

  • They described the observable impact of electric charges and magnetic fields close to the source but failed to describe the distant impact of these forces. 它们描述了靠近源头的电荷和磁场的可观影响，但未能描述这些力的远程影响。

  • In the static case, when all electric charges are permanently fixed or if they all move at a steady state, the electric field and the magnetic field are not interconnected. 在静态情况下，当所有电荷永久固定或者它们都以稳定状态运动时，电场和磁场是不相互连接的。

  • This allows us to study electricity and magnetism as two distinct and separate phenomena. 这使我们能够将电和磁视为两个独立且分离的现象。

<3> Maxwell Theory

  • Maxwell showed that any conductor (e.g., an antenna) supplied with an alternating current produces a varying magnetic field (H-field), which, in turn, produces electric field lines (E-field) in space. 麦克斯韦表明，任何导体（例如，天线）在提供交流电流时产生变化的磁场（H场），进而在空间中产生电场线（E场）。

  • This is termed the near field. In the near field, both the E- and H-fields are relatively static with no propagation. They only vary in strength as the current varies, with the magnetic flux of the H-field coming out from the antenna, and going back in, and the E-field emanating outward. 这被称为近场。在近场中，E场和H场都是相对静态的，没有传播。它们的强度随电流变化而变化，其中H场的磁通量从天线中出来并返回，而E场向外辐射。

  • Maxwell also proved that beyond this quasi-static near field, both the E-fields and H-fields at a certain distance, detached themselves from the conductor and propagated into free space as a combined wave, moving at the speed of light with a constant ratio of E/H = 120π or 377 ohms. (Ohms are used because the E-field is measured in volts per meter and the H-field in amperes per meter.) The point in which this happens is called the far field. 麦克斯韦还证明，在这个准静态近场之外，一定距离处的E场和H场与导体分离，并以E/H = 120π或377欧姆的恒定比率作为联合波传播到自由空间中，以光速移动。（欧姆用于E场以伏特每米和H场以安培每米测量。）这种情况发生的点被称为远场。

<4> Maxwell Equations

  • The electromagnetic waves are generated by the movement of electrical charges such as in a conductive metal object or antenna. 电磁波是由电荷移动产生的，例如在导电金属物体或天线中。

  • Maxwell’s first two equations contain constants of proportionality that dictate the strengths of the fields. They are normally expressed relative to the values in free space:
    Maxwell的前两个方程包含比例常数，决定了场的强度。它们通常相对于自由空间中的值表示：

    • Permeability of the medium (in Henries per meter): , 媒质的磁导率（亨利每米）
    • Permittivity of the medium (in farads per meter): 媒质的介电常数（法拉每米）

<5> Near-Field and Far-Field

  • By applying the Maxwell’s equation to magnetic dipoles, we can identify that the distance is of significance in determining the nature of the fields surrounding the dipoles. 通过将麦克斯韦方程应用于磁偶极子，我们可以确定距离对于确定周围场的性质具有重要意义。
    • Within this distance, we have the near-field region; 在这个距离范围内，我们有近场区域；
    • Beyond this distance, the far-field region starts. 超过这个距离，远场区域开始。
  • Maxwell’s equations correctly describe both the energy storage field and the energy propagation field.
    Maxwell方程式正确描述了能量存储场和能量传播场。

(3) RF Spectrum and Electromagnetic Signal Transmission RF频谱与电磁信号传输

  • Electromagnetic waves are energy and momentum transmitted as waves caused by same-phase oscillation between mutual perpendicular electric field and magnetic field.
    电磁波是由相互垂直的电场和磁场之间的同相振荡引起的能量和动量传输的波。

(4) Materials as Reflectors, Absorbers 材料作为反射体和吸收体

  • Conducting materials, such as metals, act similar to perfect reflectors for UHF radiation. 金属等导电材料对UHF辐射起到类似完美反射体的作用。

  • Materials such as glass, concrete, and cardboard are effectively RF transparent for waves that are incident upon them with an angle of incidence of 90°, but they become less transparent as the angle of incidence becomes more oblique. 玻璃、混凝土和纸板等材料在入射角为90°时对射入的波有效地具有射频透明性，但随着入射角变得更加倾斜，它们的透明性会降低。

  • Some materials, such as water, act as both good reflectors of electromagnetic waves and good attenuators, or absorbers, of electromagnetic energy. 一些材料，如水，既是电磁波的良好反射体，又是电磁能量的良好衰减体或吸收体。

2. RF SPECTRUM AND ELECTROMAGNETIC SIGNAL TRANSMISSION射频频谱和电磁信号的传输

(1) Electromagnetic Waves 电磁波

  • Propagation vector or Poynting vector: is the power flow density of the EM wave, vector P = E × H in the direction of propagation, and measured in watts per square meter.

    传播向量或波因廷矢量：是电磁波的功率流密度，向量P = E × H在传播方向上，以每平方米瓦特为单位测量。

  • Electromagnetic field: can be described in terms of the electric and/or magnetic field strength at that location.

    电磁场：可以用该位置的电场和/或磁场强度来描述。

  • Like any wave-related phenomenon, electromagnetic energy can be characterized by a wavelength and/or frequency.

    像任何与波相关的现象一样，电磁能量可以通过波长和/或频率来表征。

    • Frequency f: is defined as a number of cycles, or periods, per unit of time and is measured in hertz (Hz).

      频率f：定义为单位时间内的周期数或周期数，并以赫兹（Hz）为单位测量。

    • The wavelength λ: is the distance by which the phase of the sinusoidal wave changes by 2π radians, so we can say: kλ = 2π, ω = kc, and f = ω/2π. 波长λ：是正弦波的相位改变2π弧度的距离，因此我们可以说：kλ = 2π，ω = kc，并且f = ω/2π。

    • Angular frequency ω: is a rotational unit of angular frequency 2πf with units in radians per second (rad/s).

      角频率ω：是角频率2πf的旋转单位，单位为弧度每秒（rad/s）。

3. SIGNAL VOLTAGE AND ENERGY

(1) Signal Voltage and Energy 信号电压和能量

  • The changing electric filed can be described by the function of voltage or current.
    不断变化的电磁场可以用电压或电流的函数来描述。

  • The magnitude of the energy is expressed by the power. 能量的大小是用功率来表示。

  • The electric field varying according to the sinusoidal law, can be calculated as one cycle of current energy by calculus and divided by time, so as to obtain the average power. 电场根据正弦定律变化，可以通过微积分计算出电流能量的一个周期，并除以时间，从而得到平均功率。

  • The relative change is more worthy of attention in the signal processing problem. 相对变化在信号处理问题中更值得注意。

  • Use decibels (dB) instead of watts (W). 使用分贝（dB）代替瓦特(W)。

  • Decibels are relative. 分贝是相对的。

  • The reference power is needed when describing the specific power. 在描述比功率时，需要使用参考功率。

    • Commonly used is 1mW. Here the dB size is dBm. 常用的是1mW。这里的dB大小是dBm。

(5) Basic Antenna Parameters

  • A transmitting antenna is a device used to transform an RF signal, traveling on a conductor, into an electromagnetic wave in free space. 发送天线是一种用于将沿导体传播的射频信号转换为自由空间中的电磁波的设备。
  • A receiving antenna performs an inverse function. 接收天线执行逆向功能。
  • Antennas demonstrate a property known as reciprocity, which means that an antenna will maintain the same characteristics regardless if it is transmitting or receiving. 天线表现出一种称为互易性的特性，这意味着无论天线是发送还是接收，它都将保持相同的特性。
  • Most antennas are passive resonant devices, which operate efficiently over a relatively narrow frequency band. 大多数天线是被动谐振设备，它们在相对较窄的频带上有效运行。

1. CHECKSUM PROCEDURE

◼ checksum 校验和

– recognize transmission errors 识别传输错误

◼ The most common checksum procedures

– Parity checks 奇偶校验

– LRC

– CRC

  • Interference during transmission can lead to errors in the data 干扰可能导致数据错误

(1) Parity checking

– A parity bit is incorporated into each byte 每个字节中都包含一个奇偶校验位
– 9 bits are sent for every byte 每个字节发送9位
– odd parity: an odd number of the 9 bits have the value 1 奇校验：9位中有奇数个1
– even parity: an even number of the 9 bits have the value 1 偶校验：9位中有偶数个1
– simple
– poor error recognition 误差识别较差

  • an odd number of inverted bits (1,3,5, …) : detected 有奇数个反转位（1、3、5…）：可检测到
  • an even number of inverted bits (2,4,6,…) : the errors cancel each other out, appear to be correct. 有偶数个反转位（2、4、6…）：错误会相互抵消，看起来是正确的。

– odd parity : E5h, 1110 0101 p=0.
– a parity generator for even parity: the XOR logic gating of all the data bits in a byte 用于偶校验的奇偶校验生成器：对字节中的所有数据位进行异或逻辑门操作
– odd parity: invert the output 奇校验：反转输出

The parity of a byte can be determined by performing multiple exclusive OR logic gating operations on the individual bits 一个字节的奇偶性可以通过对每个位执行多个排他性或逻辑门控操作来确定

(2) LRC Procedure

  • LRC: Longitudinal redundancy check 纵向冗余校验

  • The XOR checksum is generated by the recursive XOR gating of all the data bytes in a data block. XOR校验和由数据块中所有数据字节的递归XOR门操作生成。

  • Byte 1 is XOR gated with byte 2, the outcome of this gating is XOR gated with byte 3, and so on. 字节1与字节2进行XOR门操作，该操作的结果与字节3进行XOR门操作，以此类推。

  • If the LRC value is appended to a data block and transmitted with it, then a simple check for transmission errors can be performed in the receiver by generating an LRC from the data block + LRC byte. 如果将LRC值附加到数据块并与其一起传输，则接收器可以通过从数据块+LRC字节生成LRC来进行简单的传输错误检查。

  • The result of this operation must always be zero; any other result indicates that transmission errors have occurred.

    此操作的结果必须始终为零；任何其他结果表明发生了传输错误。

  • Can be calculated very simply and quickly 可以非常简单快速地计算

  • Not very reliable because it is possible for multiple errors to cancel each other out, and the check cannot detect whether bytes have been transposed within a data block 不太可靠，因为多个错误可能会互相抵消，而且检查无法检测出数据块内的字节是否已经转置

  • Primarily used for the rapid checking of very small data blocks (e.g. 32 byte) 主要用于快速检查非常小的数据块（例如32字节）

  • 注：正常使用41，如果发生变动会不是41，但是只能检测奇数变动

(3) CRC Procedure

  • CRC (cyclic redundancy check) procedure: originally used in disk drives CRC（循环冗余校验）过程：最初用于磁盘驱动

  • Excellently suited for error recognition 非常适合于误差识别

  • Highly reliable method of recognizing errors 高度可靠的误差识别方法

  • Cannot correct errors 不能纠正错误

  • A CRC checksum is calculated by the division of a polynomial using a so-called generator polynomial.

    CRC校验和是通过使用所谓的生成多项式进行多项式除法计算得到的。

  • The CRC value is the remainder obtained from this division. CRC值是从这个除法得到的余数。

    • The first byte of the data block is F7h=11110111
    • The generator polynomial is = 10011
    • The starting value: 0000B

  • Transmitter: calculate the CRC value, append to data block 传输者：计算CRC值，附加到数据块中

  • Receiver: calculate the CRC value of the received data, including the appended CRC byte 接收器：计算接收到的数据的CRC值，包括附加的CRC字节

  • The result is always zero, unless transmission errors 结果始终为零，除非发生传输错误

  • Both CRC calculations start from the same initial value 两个CRC计算都从相同的初始值开始

  • Advantage: reliability of error recognition 优点：误差识别的可靠性

  • A 16-bit CRC: checking the data integrity of data blocks up to 4Kbytes in length
    16位CRC：检查长达4K字节的数据块的数据完整性

  • RFID systems: data blocks, shorter than 4Kbytes, 12- and 8-bit CRCs

    RFID系统：数据块，长度小于4K字节，使用12位和8位CRC

(3) Frame Slotted ALOHA Algorithm

  • Based on S-ALOHA, several time slots are organized into one frame, and the reader identify tags by frame.

  1. Reader broadcast frame length (f). （读取器广播帧长度（f）。）
  2. After receiving “f”, the tag randomly selects an integer from 0~(f-1) as its slot number and stores it in the register (SN).
    （接收到“f”后，标签随机选择0~(f-1)之间的整数作为其时间槽号，并将其存储在寄存器（SN）中。）
  3. In each time slot of the next frame, the reader starts a new time slot by the slot start command, and if the tag’s SN is 0, the identifier is immediately sent; otherwise, SN-1. （在下一个帧的每个时间槽中，读取器通过时间槽开始命令启动一个新的时间槽，如果标签的SN为0，则立即发送标识符；否则，SN-1。）
  4. If the identifier is sent successfully, the tag goes asleep; otherwise, it waits for the next frame to reselect a slot to send the identifier. （如果标识符发送成功，则标签进入睡眠状态；否则，它等待下一个帧重新选择一个时间槽来发送标识符。）

4. TAG-DRIVEN COLLISION ALGORITHM (Random BINARY TREE)

The basic idea of Binary tree based anti-collision algorithm: The conflicting tag set is divided into two subsets in a recursive way until only one tag is left in the set. 冲突的标记集以递归方式划分为两个子集，直到集中只剩下一个标记。

Algorithm for dividing subsets:

  • (Random) Binary Tree (BT) algorithm: Let tags randomly select the sets to which they belong.
    （随机二叉树（BT）算法：让标签随机选择它们所属的集合。）
  • (Binary) Query Tree (QT) algorithm: Divide subsets according to tags’ identifier.
    （二叉查询树（QT）算法：根据标签的标识符划分子集。）

(1) Random binary tree based anti-collision algorithm:

  • Random binary tree based anti-collision algorithm requires that each tag maintains a counter (initial value is 0). 基于随机二叉树的反碰撞算法要求每个标签都维护一个计数器（初始值为0）。
  • At the beginning of each slot, if the tag’s counter is 0, it immediately sends its identifier; otherwise, it does not respond. 在每个插槽的开始，如果标签的计数器是0，它立即发送其标识符，否则它不响应。

  • In general, the tag will enter the silent state and will not respond to the reader’s command any more after it is successfully identified. 一般情况下，标签将进入静默状态，并且在成功识别后将不再响应读卡器的命令。

  • In-field tags adjust the counter rule: If the slot is a collision slot, the corresponding tag will randomly select 0 or 1 and add to its own counter. 域内标签调整计数器规则：如果slot是一个冲突slot，相应的标签将随机选择0或1并添加到自己的计数器。

  • The whole identification process is like a binary tree with pre-order traversal.
    整个识别过程就像一个具有预定顺序遍历的二叉树。

  • There is no problem of starvation, but the counter needs to be maintained. 没有饥饿的问题，但计数器的值需要维持。

5. READER-DRIVEN ANTI-COLLISION ALGORITHMS(Binary Query Tree(Polling))

  • Binary query tree based algorithm is a stateless protocol, and the tag only needs to be compared to the identifier prefix broadcasted by the reader. 基于二进制查询树的算法是一种无状态协议，标签只需要与阅读器广播的标识符前缀进行比较
  • The reader maintains a binary prefix (initial value is 0). At the beginning of each slot, the reader broadcasts the binary prefix, which compares with the first few digits of the tag identifier, and if they are the same, then the tag sends the identifier.
    读取器将维护一个二进制前缀（初始值为0）。在每个插槽的开始，读取器广播二进制前缀，它与标签标识符的前几位数字进行比较，如果它们是相同的，则标签发送标识符。
  • The whole identification process is like creating a query binary tree based on tags’ identifier. 整个识别过程就像基于标签的标识符创建一个查询二叉树。
  • Can be used for tags without writable storage area. Tags have no starvation problem. It will be affected by ID length and distribution. 可用于没有可写存储区域的标记。标签没有饥饿的问题。它将受到ID长度和分布的影响

  • Examples of query binary tree algorithms:详细解释可以看教材P100页

6. READER-DRIVEN ANTI-COLLISION ALGORITHMS(Binary Search based)

(1) Binary Search

<1> 先知

  • Requires: the precise bit position of a data collision is recognized in the reader. 需要：读取器能够识别数据冲突的精确位位置。
  • Compares: the collision behavior of NRZ (non-return-to-zero) and Manchester coding. 比较：NRZ（非归零）和曼彻斯特编码的冲突行为。

  • NRZ code:

    • The reader cannot detect whether the sequence of received bitsare due to: 读取器无法检测所接收到的比特序列是否由于
      • the superposition of transmissions from several transponders 来自几个应答器的传输的叠加
      • or the signal from a single transponder. 还是来自单个转发器的信号。

  • Manchester code:

    • If two transponders simultaneously transmit bits of different values: 如果两个转发器同时发送不同值的位
      • the positive and negative transitions cancel each other out. 则正负转换会互相抵消
      • a subcarrier signal received for the duration of an entire bit. 在整个位的持续时间内接收到一个子载波信号。

<2> 状态-命令

  • REQUEST(SNR):
    • Sends a serial number to transponder. 向转发器发送一个序列号。
    • If the transponder’s own serial number is less than (or equal to) the received serial number: sends its own serial number back. 如果转发器自己的序列号小于（或等于）接收到的序列号：发送自己的序列号回来。

  • SELECT_(SNR):

    • Sends a serial number to transponder. 向转发器发送一个序列号。
    • The transponder with the identical address process other commands. (selected)
      具有相同地址的转发器处理其他命令。
    • Transponders with different addresses only respond to a REQUEST command.
      仅具有不同地址的转发器响应请求命令。

  • READ_DATA:

    • The selected transponder sends stored data to the reader. 选定的转发器将存储的数据发送给读取器。

  • UNSELECT:

    • The selection of a previously selected transponder is cancelled and the transponder is ‘muted’ (completely inactive).
      取消之前选定的转发器的选择，将转发器“静音”（完全不活动）。

<3>Examples：

  • the transponders: possess unique serial numbers in the range 00-FFh (=0-255 dec. or 00000000 – 11111111 bin. )
    应答器：具有00-FFh范围的唯一序列号。
  • Reader: REQUEST (≤ 11111111)
  • All transponders must be less than or equal to 11111111b.
  • Answered by all transponders. 所有转发器都会回答。
  • Precise synchronization, bit position of a collision. 精确的同步，冲突的位位置。
  • Bit 0, bit 4, and bit 6: there is a collision (X). 位0、位4和位6：存在冲突（X）。
  • the received bit sequence 1X1X001X : eight possibilitiesfor the serial numbers
    接收到的位序列1X1X001X：序列号的八种可能性
  • bit 6 : at least one transponder both in the range SNR ≥ 11000000b and also in SNR ≤ 10111111b.
    位6：至少一个在信噪比≥11000000b和信噪比≤101111111b范围内的应答器。
  • search in the range ≤ 10111111b: set bit 6 equal to 0 (highest value bit with collision), and ignore all lower value bits by setting them to 1.
    在≤10111111b范围内搜索：将位6设置为0（带有碰撞的最高值位），并通过将其设置为1来忽略所有低值位。
  • reader : REQUEST (≤ 10111111)
  • transponders that fulfill this condition : respond by sending their own serial numbers to the reader 满足此条件的应答器：通过发送自己的序列号来响应
  • transponders 1, 2, 3
  • collision (X) : at bit 0 and bit 4 of the received number
  • reader : REQUEST (≤ 10101111).
  • Possible serial numbers in the search range after the evaluation of the second iteration. The transponders marked () are actually present: 第二次迭代评估后搜索范围内可能的序列号。标记有（）的应答器确实存在：
  • only fulfilled by transponder 2 (10100011), which now responds to the command alone. 只有应答器2（10100011）完成，现在只响应命令。
  • SELECT command: transponder 2 is selected
  • Transponder 2 can now be read or written by the reader.
  • All other transponders are silent.

(2) Dynamic Binary Search based Anti-Collision Algorithm

  • Binary search procedure: both the search criterion and the serial numbers are always transmitted at full length.
    二分搜索过程：搜索准则和序列号始终以全长度传输。
  • Bits (X-1) to 0 of the command contain no additional information, always set to 1.
    命令的位（X-1）到0不包含任何额外信息，始终设为1。
  • Bits N to X of the serial number in the transponder’s response contain no additional information for the reader.
    应答器响应中序列号的N到X位不包含阅读器的附加信息。
  • The reader: sends only the known part (N − X) as the search criterion. 读取器：只发送已知部分（N - X）作为搜索准则。
  • Transponders that correspond to the search criterion in the bits (N − X): transmit the remaining bits, (X −1) to 0.
    与位（N - X）中的搜索准则相对应的转发器：传输剩余的位，（X - 1）到0。
  • NVB (number of valid bits): inform transponders of the number of subsequent bits. NVB（有效位数）：通知转发器后续位数的数量
