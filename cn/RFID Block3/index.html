Lecture 7: EPCGlobal Standard & Protocol

1. Standardization

  • What is a standard?

    • Mandatory or discretionary rules, requirements, or conditions 强制或自由裁量的规则、要求或条件
    • Performance, design, operation, or measurements of quality 性能、设计、操作或质量测量

  • Why do we need standards?

    • Better interoperability 更好的互操作性
    • Lower costs

  • The essence of RFID standards RFID标准的本质

    • ‘Air interface’ protocol 空中接口协议

  • Standardization Bodies（部分）

2. Standardization Types

  1. Animal identification

  2. Contactless smart card

    a. ISO/IEC 10536 – close-coupling smart cards

    b. ISO/IEC 14443 – proximity-coupling smart cards

    c. ISO/IEC 15693 – vicinity-coupling smart cards

  3. ISO/IEC 10374 – Container identification

  4. Item management – ISO/IEC 18000 series

(1) Animal Identification

  • ISO standards 11784, 11785 and 14223 deal with the identification of animals using RFID systems.

    • ISO/IEC 11784: Radio-frequency identification of animals – Code structure

    • ISO/IEC 11785: Radio-frequency identification of animals – Technical concept

    • ISO/IEC 14223: Radio-frequency identification of animals – Advanced transponders

  • The constructional form of the transponder used is not specified in the standards and therefore the form can be designed to suit the animal in question. 标准中没有指定使用的应答器的结构形式，因此可以根据具体的动物设计形式。

  • Small, sterile glass transponders that can be injected into the fatty tissues are normally used for the identification of cows, horses and sheep. 小型无菌玻璃应答器通常用于识别牛、马和羊，这些应答器可以注射到脂肪组织中。

    • Ear tags or collars are also possible. 耳标或项圈也是可能的。

<1> ISO/IEC 11784 – Code Structure

  • The identification code for animals comprises a total of 64 bits (8 bytes). 动物的识别码总共包括 64 位（8 字节）。

位数 信息 描述
1 动物（1）/ 非动物应用（0） 指定应答器是用于动物识别还是用于其他用途
2–15 保留 为将来的应用保留
16 数据块（1）/ 无数据块（0） 指定在识别码之后是否会传输附加数据
17–26 国家代码（依据ISO/IEC 3166） 指定使用国家（代码999表示测试应答器）
27–64 国家识别码 唯一的、特定国家的注册号码

<2> ISO/IEC 11785 – Technical Concept

  • Reader’s operating frequency: 134.2 ± 1.8 kHz. 读写器的工作频率：134.2 ± 1.8 kHz
  • The emitted field provides a power supply for the transponder and is therefore termed the ‘activation field’. 发射的场为应答器提供电源，因此称为“激活场”
  • The activation field is periodically switched on for 50 ms at a time and then switched off for 3 ms. 激活场周期性地开启50毫秒，然后关闭3毫秒。

​

  • During the 50 ms period when it is switched on it waits for the response from a full/half-duplex transponder – a sequential transponder in the field requires the activation field to charge up its charging capacitor. 在开启的50毫秒期间，它等待全双工/半双工应答器的响应——在场中的顺序应答器需要激活场来给它的充电电容充电。

  • If a full/half-duplex transponder is present within the range of the activation field, then this transponder sends its data during the operating interval of the field. 如果在激活场范围内有全双工/半双工应答器存在，那么该应答器会在场的工作间隔内发送其数据。当接收到数据时，如果数据传输没有在前50 ms内完成，则操作间隔可以延长到100 ms。

  • A sequential transponder in the range of the activation field begins to transmit data within the 3 ms pause. The duration of the pause is extended to a maximum of 20 ms to permit the complete transmission of a data record. 在激活场范围内的顺序应答器在3毫秒的暂停期间开始传输数据。暂停的持续时间延长至最长20毫秒，以允许完整传输数据记录。

  • 100毫秒(最大)的激活场时段提供了一个更长的持续激活时间窗口，以确保所有应答器可以充分充电和准备数据传输。这对于系统的稳定性和数据传输的可靠性非常重要

<3> ISO/IEC 14223 – Advanced Transponders

  • This standard defines the RF interface and the data structure of so-called advanced transponders. 该标准定义了所谓的高级转发器的射频接口和数据结构。
  • ISO/IEC 14223 is based upon the older standards ISO/IEC 11784 and ISO/IEC 11785 and represents a further development of these standards. ISO/IEC 14223 基于较旧的标准 ISO/IEC 11784 和 ISO/IEC 11785，代表了这些标准的进一步发展。
  • The standard consists of three parts:
    • Part 1: ‘Air Interface’, 空中接口
    • Part 2: ‘Code and Command Structure’ and 代码和命令结构
    • Part 3: ‘Applications’. 应用程序

(2) Contactless Smart Card

  • There are currently three different standards for contactless smart cards based upon a broad classification of the range 目前，基于范围的广泛分类，非接触式智能卡有三种不同的标准

​

  • ISO/IEC 10536：近耦合卡，操作范围为0到1厘米，适用于非常近距离的应用。（1992-1995）
  • ISO/IEC 14443：近距离耦合卡，操作范围为0到10厘米，常用于支付卡和门禁卡等应用。
  • ISO/IEC 15693：远距离耦合卡，操作范围为0到1米，适用于需要较远距离读取的应用，如资产跟踪和门禁系统。

(3) ISO/IEC 10374 Container Identification

  • This standard describes an automatic identification system for containers based upon microwave transponders. 这个标准描述了基于微波应答器的容器自动识别系统
  • The optical identification of containers is described in the standard ISO/IEC 6346 and is reflected in the data record of the transponder-based container identification. 容器的光学识别描述在标准ISO/IEC 6346中，并反映在基于应答器的容器识别的数据记录中
  • Active – i.e. battery supported – microwave transponders are used. These are activated by an unmodulated carrier signal in the frequency ranges 850–950 and 2400–2500MHz. 主动型（即电池支持的）微波应答器被使用。这些应答器通过频率范围在850–950 MHz和2400–2500 MHz的未调制载波信号激活。
  • The sensitivity of the transponder is defined with an electric field strength E of a maximum of 150 mV/m. 应答器的灵敏度定义为最大电场强度E为150 mV/m
    • 电场强度越低，灵敏度越高。灵敏度是指设备检测和响应微弱信号的能力。如果一个应答器能够在非常低的电场强度下（即接收到的信号非常弱的情况下）仍然能够有效地工作，那么我们就说这个应答器的灵敏度很高。
  • The transponder responds by backscatter modulation (modulated reflection cross-section), using a modified FSK subcarrier procedure. 应答器通过反向散射调制（调制反射截面）做出响应，使用改进的FSK副载波程序
  • The signal is modulated between the two subcarrier frequencies 40 and 20 kHz. 信号在两个副载波频率 40 kHz 和 20 kHz 之间调制。

​

  • '1’位：频率从高频40 kHz切换到低频20 kHz。

  • '0’位：频率从低频20 kHz切换到高频40 kHz。

(4) Item Management

  • A whole range of standards about item management is now available. These newer standards fit smoothly into the large number of older standards that were developed based on barcodes. The following standards are relevant for RFID: 现在有一整套关于项目管理的标准。这些较新的标准可以顺利地适应基于条形码开发的大量旧标准。以下标准与 RFID 相关：
    • ISO/IEC 15961: ‘RFID for Item Management: Host Interrogator; Tag functional commands and other syntax features’
      用于项目管理的 RFID：主机询问器;标签功能命令和其他语法特征
    • ISO/IEC 15962: ‘RFID for Item Management: Data Syntax’ 用于项目管理的 RFID：数据语法
    • ISO/IEC 15963: ‘Unique Identification of RF tag and Registration Authority to manage the uniqueness’
      射频标签的唯一标识和管理唯一性的注册机构
    • ISO/IEC 18000: ‘RFID for Item Management: Air Interface’ 用于项目管理的 RFID：空中接口
    • ISO/IEC 18001: ‘Information technology – RFID for Item Management – Application Requirements Profiles’
      信息技术 - 用于项目管理的 RFID - 应用需求配置文件

3. EPCglobal standard

(1) 介绍

  • UHF RFID protocol standards are mainly developed by two organizations: EPCglobal，ISO

  • Two users of the standard : Walmart，美国国防部

  • In 1999, EPCs started when the Auto-ID-Center began to develop the necessary technology and network architecture.
    1999 年，当 Auto-ID-Center 开始开发必要的技术和网络架构时，EPC 开始了。

    • Goal: create a cost-efficient, global and open-source system 目标：创建一个具有成本效益的、全球性的开源系统
    • Develop less complex original standards for the communication between transponder and reader 为应答器和读卡器之间的通信制定不太复杂的原始标准
    • Can be implemented more cost-efficiently than existing RFID standards 可以比现有的RFID标准更具成本效益地实施

  • A partnership between almost 100 global companies and seven of the world’s leading research universities 近 100 家全球公司与七所世界领先的研究型大学之间的合作伙伴关系

    • The Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the US
    • Fudan University in China

  • In 2003, European Article Number (EAN) and the Uniform Code Council (UCC) founded EPCglobal (converted from Auto-ID Center), a worldwide, open nonprofit organization for the international implementation of EPCglobal **Network **
    2003 年，EAN 和UCC 成立了 EPCglobal（由自动识别中心转换而来），这是一个全球性的开放非营利组织，用于国际实施 EPCglobal 网络

  • Mission 任务 （EPCglobal） 三点：RFID和EPC+代码注册+ISO

    • Develop and oversee the standards of electronic product codes (EPC) by using the RFID technology
      使用 RFID 技术制定和监督电子产品代码 （EPC） 标准
    • Provide registry service of the codes 提供代码注册服务
    • Coordinate with ISO for their review and ratification 与ISO协调审查和批准

(2) EPCGlobal Network

<1>

  • The EPCglobal Network is a technology that

    • Allows trading partners to document and determine the location of individual goods
      EPC全球网络是一项允许贸易伙伴记录和确定单个货物的位置的技术
    • If possible, in real time 如果可能，实时

  • Additional information 其他信息：

    • Production and use-by date of a product 产品的生产和保质期
    • Can be exchanged between trading partners 可以在贸易伙伴之间交换

  • The EPCglobal Network

    • Uses the Internet 使用互联网
    • Adds specific services: Object Naming Service (ONS) , EPC Information Service (EPCIS) 添加特定服务：对象命名服务 （ONS）、EPC 信息服务 （EPCIS）

  • Objects: registered with RFID readers 对象：在RFID阅读器中注册

  • Readers: connected via a software interface(中间件) with the Internet services of EPCglobal Network 读卡器：通过软件接口与EPCglobal Network的互联网服务连接

  • Combination of

    • Data acquisition via RFID technology 通过RFID技术采集数据
    • Providing, distributing and connecting these data via existing Internet technology 通过现有的互联网技术提供、分发和连接这些数据

  • Offers a large potential for improved efficiency and precise logistics processes in national and international trading 为提高国内和国际贸易的效率和精确的物流流程提供了巨大的潜力

<2> EPCglobal网络架构

  1. RFID层：

    • Tag（标签）：这是RFID标签，附着在物品上，用于存储电子产品代码（EPC）。
    • Reader（读卡器）：用于读取RFID标签上的EPC信息，并将信息传输到本地网络。

  2. 本地网络层：也叫中间件层，是接口

    • Reader（读卡器）：连接到本地网络，负责读取标签信息并将数据传输到本地服务器。
    • Local Server（包括Savant）：本地服务器处理来自读卡器的数据，可能包括数据过滤、聚合和临时存储等功能。Savant是一个中间件组件，用于处理和管理RFID数据。
    • Database（数据库）：存储和管理从RFID系统收集的数据。
    • Computer（计算机）：用户可以通过计算机访问和管理RFID数据。

  3. 互联网层：

    • Router（路由器）：将本地网络连接到互联网，使得RFID数据可以传输到更高级的网络层次。
    • Internet（互联网）：作为全球通信网络，连接本地网络和EPCglobal网络的服务器。
    • ONS Server（对象命名服务服务器）：提供EPC到网络地址的映射服务，允许用户查找EPC相关信息的网络位置。
    • EPCIS Server（EPC信息服务服务器）：存储和管理关于EPC事件的数据，如物品的时间、地点和状态信息，供企业之间共享和查询。

工作流程

  1. RFID标签和读卡器交互：读卡器读取标签上的EPC信息。
  2. 数据传输到本地服务器：读卡器将读取的信息传输到本地服务器进行处理和临时存储。
  3. 本地服务器处理数据：Savant中间件组件处理数据，并将其存储在本地数据库中。
  4. 数据传输到互联网：通过路由器将数据传输到互联网。
  5. 数据访问和共享：通过互联网，数据可以被传输到ONS和EPCIS服务器，提供全球范围的EPC信息服务和数据共享。

<2> 基本服务

  • The EPCglobal Network defines and uses the following five basic services

  1. Electronic product code (EPC): a unique number for identifying individual objects 电子产品代码 （EPC）：用于识别单个对象的唯一编号

  2. **The identification system ** 识别系统

    • Transponder: attached to products , contains the EPC 应答器：附在产品上，包含EPC
    • Reader: read out the EPC, route it via EPC middleware into the network 读卡器：读出EPC，通过EPC中间件路由到网络

  3. EPCglobal Middleware: administrates the information made available by readers, a software interface EPCglobal 中间件：管理读者提供的信息，一个软件接口

  4. Discovery Service (DS): a group of services 发现服务 （DS）：一组服务

    • Allow users to find data regarding a certain EPC in the EPCglobal Network 允许用户在 EPCglobal 网络中查找有关特定 EPC 的数据
    • Object Naming Service (ONS) 对象命名服务 （ONS）

  5. EPC Information Services (EPCIS) EPC 信息服务 （EPCIS）

    • Allow users to exchange EPC-related data via EPCglobal 允许用户通过 EPCglobal 交换 EPC 相关数据
    • Network with their trading partners 与贸易伙伴建立联系

<5> Gen2 Protocol

  • Taking on the standardization work of the Auto-ID Center, EPCglobal began to develop a new generation of communication standards between transponder and reader. 承接Auto-ID中心的标准化工作，EPCglobal开始开发新一代的应答器和读写器之间的通信标准。

  • Gen2 Protocol

    • A uniform, globally valid protocol 一个统一的、全球有效的协议
    • Not backwards compatible with the previous Auto-ID-Center standard 不向后兼容之前的Auto-ID-Center标准

  • Advantages of Gen2 Protocol

    • A specific dense reader mode: prevents adjacent readers from interfering 一种特定的密集读写器模式：防止相邻读写器干扰

    • An improved segmentation of the memory supplied by the transponder: four independent sectors 改进的应答器提供的内存分段：四个独立的扇区

    • Migration of barcode applications to transponders is facilitated: several transponders can get an identical EPC, treat transponders virtually as barcodes 便于将条形码应用迁移到应答器：多个应答器可以获得相同的EPC，几乎将应答器视为条形码

    • A changed bit coding on the RF interface: improves the detection rate of transponders 改变了RF接口上的位编码：提高了应答器的检测率

<6> EPC

  • EPC: Electronic Product Code

    • Naming and identification scheme 命名和识别方案
    • Enable the unique identification of all objects 实现对所有物体的唯一识别

  • Combination of three unique numbers 三个唯一号码的组合

    • Domain Manager number 域管理号码
    • Object Class number 对象类号码
    • Serial number 序列号

  • Domain Manager number + Object Class number + Serial Number: uniquely identifies an object

  • EPC: Electronic Product Code

    • Version Number: indicates the number of bits contained in each of the remaining three partitions 版本号：表明其余三个分区中每个包含的位数
    • Domain Manager
    • Object Class
    • Serial Number

​

  • 例子：EPC-96 type I : 01 0000A89 00016F 000169DC0 （这里是16进制，1位等于二进制的4位）
    • 01 – Version Number (8 bits)
    • 0000A89 – Domain Manager(28 bits)
    • 00016F – Object Class (24bits)
    • 000169DC0 – Serial Number (36bits)

​

(3) EPCGlobal Classes

  • Transponders: classified according to functionality 应答器：根据功能分类

  • There are the following classes:

  1. Class 0: Passive read-only transponder with 64-bit or 96-bit EPC.
    第0类： 64位或96位EPC的无源只读应答器。

  2. Class 1: Passive transponders, write once read many with 64-bit or 96-bit EPC. (WORM)
    第1类： 64位或96位EPC的无源应答器，写一次多次读取。

    • A ‘kill’ function that permanently disables the Tag 具有永久禁用标签的“杀死”功能
    • Optional password-protected access control 可选的密码保护访问控制
    • Optional user memory 可选的用户内存

  3. Class 2: Passive transponders with the following anticipated features above and beyond those of Class 1 tags 第2类： 无源应答器，具有以下预期功能，超出第1类标签的功能

    • An extended Tag ID 扩展的标签ID
    • Extended user memory 扩展的用户内存
    • Authenticated access control 认证的访问控制
    • Additional features (TBD) as will be defined in the Class 2 specification 附加功能（待定），将在第2类规范中定义

  4. Class 3: Semi-passive transponders with the following anticipated features above and beyond those of Class 2 tags 第3类： 具有以下预期功能的半无源应答器，超出第2类标签的功能

    • An integral power source 内置电源
    • Integrated sensing circuitry 集成感应电路

  5. Class 4: Active transponders with the following anticipated features above and beyond those of Class 3 tags 第4类： 具有以下预期功能的有源应答器，超出第3类标签的功能

    • Tag-to-Tag communications 标签到标签的通信
    • Active communications 主动通信
    • Ad-hoc networking capabilities 自组织网络功能

6. EPCGLOBAL CLASS 1 GENERATION 2 PROTOCOL

  • The operating frequency of the RFID system which complies with the EPCglobal Class 1 Generation 2 (EPC G1G2) standard is at 860MHz ~ 960MHz.

  • The EPC C1G2 standard is dedicated to providing a unified approach to reading data from RFID tags, writing data to tags, and the tag communication. EPC C1G2标准致力于提供一种统一的方法来读取RFID标签中的数据、向标签写入数据以及标签通信。

(1) Physical Layer Communication Interface

  • The physical layer communication interface in EPC C1G2 is the same as the one in the seven-layer model of the OSI protocol stack. EPC C1G2中的物理层通信接口与OSI协议栈的七层模型中的物理层相同。

  • There are two communication links in the EPC C1G2, namely, a reader-to-tag communication link and a tag-to-reader communication link. EPC C1G2中有两个通信链路，即读写器到标签的通信链路和标签到读写器的通信链路。

  • The two links are independent and have different data encoding schemes, data rates, and data modulation modes. 这两个链路是独立的，具有不同的数据编码方案、数据速率和数据调制模式。

  • The EPC C1G2 protocol standard is a half-duplex protocol that allows only one reader or one tag to send a signal in a single transmission. EPC C1G2协议标准是一种半双工协议，它只允许一个阅读器或一个标签在一次传输中发送一个信号(因此，阅读器和标签不会同时发送信号。)

  • 补充解释：

    • reader-to-tag通信链路中，阅读器通常发送命令给标签并且维持一个载波（CW）。CW是一个没有经过调制的信号，阅读时通过该信号发送能量给标签。
    • 在tag-to-reader的通信链路中，标签通过发射信号的方式响应阅读器。标签通过天线调制CW信号，并且将要响应的信号在CW中编码发送给阅读器。
    • 关于调制类型，EPCCIG2支持两种形式的调制，幅移键控(ASK)和相移键控(PSK)。在阅读器到标签的链路中，阅读器采用的是 ASK调制方式。在标签到阅读器的连读中，标签采用的是ASK或者PSK的调制方式。
    • 阅读器传输到标签的数据采用的编码方式是脉冲间隔编码(PIE)，PIE采用了两种不同长度的脉冲信号代表数据0和数据1。

(2) Tag-Identification Layer

An Interrogator manages Tag populations using three basic operations: 询问器使用三个基本操作来管理标签填充：

  1. Select: Choosing a Tag population. 选择： 选择一个标签群体。

    • An Interrogator may use a Select command to select one or more Tags based on a value or values in Tag memory. 询问器可以使用选择命令基于标签存储中的一个或多个值来选择一个或多个标签。

  2. Inventory: Identifying individual Tags. 盘点： 识别单个标签。

    • An Interrogator begins an inventory round by transmitting a Query command in one of four sessions. One or more Tags may reply. 询问器通过在四个会话中的一个会话中发送查询命令来开始盘点轮。一或多个标签可能会回复。
    • The Interrogator detects a single Tag reply and requests the Tag’s EPC. 询问器检测到一个标签回复并请求标签的EPC。
    • Inventory comprises multiple commands. 盘点包含多个命令。
    • An inventory round operates in one and only one session at a time. 一次盘点轮次只能在一个会话中进行。

  3. Access: Communicating with an identified Tag. 访问： 与已识别的标签进行通信。

    • The Interrogator may perform a core operation such as reading, writing, locking, or killing the Tag. 询问器可以执行诸如读取、写入、锁定或杀死标签等核心操作。
    • Access comprises multiple commands. 访问包含多个命令。
    • An Interrogator may only access a uniquely identified Tag. 询问器只能访问唯一识别的标签。

(3) Tag Finite State Machine 标签的有限状态机

In the EPC C1G2 protocol standard, the behavior of a tag can be described by a finite state machine. The following are the seven states of the tag: 在EPC C1G2协议标准中，标签的行为可以由有限状态机来描述。以下是该标签的七种状态：

<1> Ready state 就绪态

  • The state of the tag before it is powered up. At this point, the tag does not participate in the inquiry process. 标签在通电前的状态。此时，标签不参与查询过程。
  • In the query command sent from the reader, the tag selects a parameter to generate a random number for calculating the time slot to send. 在阅读器发送的查询命令中，标签选择一个参数生成一个随机数，用于计算发送的时间槽。
    • If it is 0, the tag enters the reply state. 如果是0，标签进入回复状态。
    • Otherwise, it enters the arbitrate state. 否则，进入仲裁状态。

<2> Arbitrate state 仲裁态

  • The tag participates in this round of inquiries. 标签参与这一轮的查询。
  • At this time the tag slot num is not 0, it will wait until the slot num is 0, and then enter the reply state. 此时标签槽号不为0，它将等待直到槽号为0，然后进入回复状态。

<3> Reply state 回复态

  • When the tag needs to reply the EPC code, it first enters the reply state. 当标签需要回复EPC代码时，它首先进入回复态。

    ​

    • 这时，标签的时隙数为0，标签返回一个16比特的随机数RN16 给阅读器。发送RN16是标签发送EPC码给阅读器的第一步。
    • 如果阅读器正确地接收到标签的随机数 RN16，它将会发送一个确认命令ACK给标签。在接收RN16的时候，阅读器可能无法正确接收，原因主要有:
      • a.两个或多个标签同时发送RN16给阅读器;
      • b.其他射频信号干扰了RN16的传输;
      • c.阅读器错过接收RN16 的时间。
    • 如果标签成功接收到来自阅读器的确认命令ACK，标签将会发送它的EPC码，同时发送PC码(描述标签的物理属性)和CRC码(用于错误检测)。

  • The tag enters the acknowledged state after receiving the ACK. Otherwise, the tag will automatically enter the arbitrate state. 标签在收到ACK后进入确认态。否则，标签将自动进入仲裁状态。

<4> Acknowledged state 确认态

  • The tag enters the acknowledged state after returning the EPC code. This is the necessary state for the tag to enter the access command. 标签在返回EPC代码后进入确认状态。这是标签进入访问命令的必要状态。
  • 在确认态中标签不会被杀死。As the reply state, if the tag does not accept the reader’s command within the specified time, it will automatically return to the arbitrate state. 就像回复态一样，如果标签在指定时间内没有收到来自阅读器的命令，它将自动返回仲裁状态。

<5> Open state 开放态

  • The state where the tag needs to be mutually exclusive when entering the access command. 开放态是标签在进入访问命令时需要互斥使用的状态。
  • If the tag does not accept the reader’s command, it will remain in this state until there is no energy. 如果标签没有接受到读写器的命令，它将保持在此状态直到没有能量为止。
  • The tag can enter the secured state from the open state. 标签可以从开放状态进入安全状态。

<6> Secured state 安全态

  • The tag can use an optional password function to require the reader to provide a protected password when executing any access command. 标签可以使用可选的密码功能，要求读写器在执行任何访问命令时提供保护正确的密码。

  • 密码的缺省值是0，这表示密码保护没有被执行或者没有被激活。

  • 将密码置为一个非零值就可以为标签提供密码保护。

  • 如果标签的密码功能被执行或者被激活，那么所有的访问命令必须从安全态中执行。而在进入安全态之前，所有的标签必须提供正确的密码。

<7> Killed state 杀死态

  • The RFID tag stores information about a particular object, killing it when it is not needed to protect the information. RFID标签存储有关特定物体的信息，当不需要保护信息时将其杀死。
  • When the tag enters the killed state, it will no longer respond to any command sent by the reader. 当标签进入杀死态时，它将不再响应读写器发送的任何命令。
  • 对于一个标签而言，接收正确密码+杀死命令之后才会进入杀死态。

(4) Read the Tag

  • The EPC C1G2 uses dynamic frame slotted ALOHA anti-collision algorithms to reduce data corruption caused by collisions. EPC C1G2使用动态帧时隙ALOHA防碰撞算法来减少碰撞引起的数据损坏。

<1> Session mechanism 会话机制

  • The RFID system must allow multiple readers to communicate with the same tag. RFID系统必须允许多个读写器与同一个标签通信。
  • Session mechanism: In the EPC C1G2 standard, up to four readers can be allowed to communicate with the tag in the same time frame. **会话机制：**在EPC C1G2标准中，最多允许四个读写器在同一时间帧内与标签通信。
  • In the four sessions of EPC C1G2, each session has its own query flag, and the other tag states are shared in each session. The four states are S0 / S1 / S2 / S3. 在EPC C1G2的四个会话中，每个会话都有自己的查询标志，其他标签状态在每个会话中共享。四种状态是S0 / S1 / S2 / S3。
  • 标记位的初始值依赖于阅读器使用的会话以及标记为以前的值当标签为激活的时候，会话S0的标记位总是被置为A;而对 S1、S2、S3 而言如果标签在有效持续时间之前被激活，则S1、S2、S3 会话的标记位则置为各自之前的值(A或者 B)，否则，标记位设为缺省值A。

  • When multiple readers read concurrently, each reader must select a different session to avoid reading the value of the flag bit between A and B repeatedly after reading the EPC code. 当多个读写器同时读取时，每个读写器必须选择不同的会话，以避免在读取EPC代码后重复读取A和B之间的标志位值。

  • Example of Two Interrogators/Readers

    • Two interrogators use inventoried flags to independently and completely inventory a common Tag population: 两个询问器使用清点的标志来独立且完整地清点一个常见的标签群体：

      • Interrogator #1 powers-on, then通电
        • Initiates an inventory round which singulates A Tags in session S2 to B启动一个清单轮，将会话 S2 中的 A 标签单选为 B
        • Powers off 关闭电源
      • Interrogator #2 powers-on, then 通电
        • Initiates an inventory round which singulates B Tags in session S3 to A 启动一个清单轮次，将会话 S3 中的 B 标签单选为 A
        • Powers off

    • This process repeats until:

      • Interrogator #1 places all Tags in session S2 into B, after which it inventories the Tags in session S2 from B back to A. 询问器 #1 将会话 S2 中的所有标签放入 B 中，然后将会话 S2 中的标签从 B 盘点回 A。
      • Interrogator #2 places all Tags in session S3 into A, after which it inventories the Tags in session S3 from A back to B. 询问器 #2 将会话 S3 中的所有标签放入 A 中，然后将会话 S3 中的标签从 A 盘点回 B。

<2> Commands to identify the tag 读取标签的命令

In the EPC C1G2 protocol, collecting the EPC code (ID) of tag is a very important operation.

I. Select Command 选择命令

  • Defines the number of tags participating in the next query process and selects the subset of tags that participate in the next round of queries. The select command may allow the reader to change the selected flag or any one of the four flags stored in the tag. 定义参与下一次查询过程的标签数量，并选择参与下一轮查询的标签子集。选择命令可能允许读写器更改所选标志或标签中存储的四个标志中的任何一个。
  • The select command contains 6 mandatory fields and an optional field 选择命令包含6个强制域和一个可选域，该可选域可以用来识别参与即将到来的一轮识别过程的标签集合。

  • 第一个强制的域是目标域(Target Field)，它允许阅读器更改选择的标记位或者四个会话中的任一个标记位。

  • 第二个强制的域是动作域(ActionField)，它指定了所有符合选择命令参数的标签行为。这些行为能够设置已选择的标记位(SLFlag)或者设置识别的标记位(InventoriesFlag)为A或者B。这些行为只有在标签的参数与选择命令中相应的内存区(MemBank)、指针(Pointer)、长度(Length)、掩码(Mask)域的参数一致时才会发生，图给出了不同情况下的标签行为。内存区、指针、长度和掩码域用来进一步指明参与识别过程的标签匹配的标准。

  • 掩码域是可选的，它的变量长度为0~255Byte。

  • 第七个域，被截断的域(TrumcateField)也是选择命令的一部分。Truncate域用来与Mask域相互协调，用来减少标签响应阅读器的确认命令(ACK)而发送的EPC码的比特数。Truncate只有在MemBank指向 EPC内存区、掩码确定、目标域指定参与识别过程的标签集合的时候才会被使用，这个特别的选择指定是标签进入识别前的最后一个指令。当截断发生作用时，标签只会回复掩码(Mask)之后的EPC码。因此Truncate 域能够用来缩短标签回复ACK命令的长度。

补充了解：

会话标记位（Session Flag）和SL标记位（Select Flag）是RFID系统中的重要控制标志，用于管理标签（Tag）和读卡器（Reader）之间的通信状态。以下是对这两个标志位的详细解释：

会话标记位用于管理RFID标签和读卡器之间的临时会话状态。在不同的会话状态下，标签可以具有不同的响应行为，从而优化多标签环境下的通信效率。常见的会话标记位包括：

  • S0, S1, S2, S3：不同的会话状态标志位，用于控制标签的抗冲突和响应行为。
    • S0：会话状态0，标签在这个状态下具有最短的持久时间，适用于标签数量较少的环境。
    • S1：会话状态1，标签在这个状态下具有中等的持久时间，适用于标签数量适中的环境。
    • S2：会话状态2，标签在这个状态下具有较长的持久时间，适用于标签数量较多的环境。
    • S3：会话状态3，标签在这个状态下具有最长的持久时间，适用于非常复杂的多标签环境。

SL标记位用于管理RFID标签的选择状态，即标签是否被选中进行进一步的通信。SL标记位的常见操作包括：

  • assert：将SL标记位设置为选中状态。
  • deassert：将SL标记位设置为未选中状态。

4. Collision Avoidance

  • Single-reader multi-tag conflict 单阅读器多标签冲突

    • Several tags send message to the reader at the same time, the reader cannot respond to any tag. 多个标签同时向阅读器发送消息，阅读器无法响应任何标签。

  • Single-tag multi-reader conflict 单标签多阅读器冲突

    • A tag receives different signals of several readers at the same time. It cannot resolve, so it can not respond to any reader. 一个标签同时接收到多个阅读器的不同信号。它不能解决，所以它不能回应任何读者。

  • Multi-reader conflict 多阅读器冲突

    • Two or more readers work in the same frequency, and they will interfere with each other, affecting the normal work 两个或两个以上的阅读器以相同的频率工作，他们会相互干扰，影响正常的工作。

(1) Tag-Tag Collision

​

  • The collision avoidance protocols that are used in the current RFID systems are often proprietary (i.e., not open-source) algorithms. Therefore, obtaining information on them is difficult. Currently, several open standards appear, and they are used more instead of proprietary solutions. 当前RFID系统中使用的防碰撞协议通常是专有（即非开源）算法。因此，获取它们的信息很困难。目前，出现了几个开放标准，它们被更多地使用以代替专有解决方案。

  • There are two main classes of collision avoidance protocols: 有两类主要的防碰撞协议：

    • Deterministic protocols: for systems using the 860–960-MHz frequency because they are more efficient in this case. A simple deterministic algorithm is the tree-walking algorithm (TWA), which is generally used in UHF readers. The HF readers use a Slotted Termination Adaptive Collection (STAC) protocol somewhat similar to the framed ALOHA protocol. 确定性协议：适用于使用860–960 MHz频率的系统，因为在这种情况下它们更高效。一个简单的确定性算法是树遍历算法（TWA），通常用于UHF读写器。HF读写器使用一种称为分时终止自适应收集（STAC）协议，有点类似于帧ALOHA协议。

    • Probabilistic protocols: for systems that use 13.56-MHz frequency (the U.S. regulations in this band offer significantly less bandwidth). The probabilistic protocols are usually based on a time-division multiple access (TDMA) protocol, called ALOHA.
      概率性协议：适用于使用13.56 MHz频率的系统（美国在这个频段的法规提供的带宽明显较少）。概率性协议通常基于一种称为ALOHA的时分多址（TDMA）协议。

(2) Reader-Tag Collision

​

  • Reader-tag collision occurs when the signal from a neighboring reader interferes with tag responses being received at another reader. 读写器-标签碰撞发生在邻近读写器的信号干扰到另一个读写器接收到的标签响应时。

  • This problem has been studied in the EPCglobal Class1 Gen1 and Gen2 standards for UHF readers. 这个问题已经在UHF读写器的EPCglobal Class1 Gen1和Gen2标准中进行了研究。

  • In Gen 1 standard, the reader-tag collision problem is mitigated by allowing frequency hopping in the UHF band or by TDMA. 在Gen 1标准中，通过允许在UHF频段内跳频或使用TDMA来缓解读写器-标签碰撞问题。

    • In Gen 2, the readers and tags operate on different frequencies so that the tag response does not interfere or collide with reader signals. 在Gen 2标准中，读写器和标签在不同频率上工作，这样标签响应就不会干扰或碰撞到读写器信号。

  • Either solution requires fairly sophisticated technology. 任一解决方案都需要相当复杂的技术。

(3) Reader-Reader Collision

​

  • In many applications such as warehousing or manufacturing, a large area must be perfectly covered. 在许多应用中，如仓储或制造业，必须完美覆盖大面积区域。

  • Several collision problems might occur when multiple readers are used in close proximity to each other. 当多个读写器在彼此靠近的地方使用时，可能会发生若干碰撞问题。

  • Colorwave is a distributed algorithm based on TDMA and one of the first works to address reader-reader collisions. Colorwave是一种基于TDMA的分布式算法，是解决读写器-读写器碰撞的首批工作之一。

  • In particular, it considers an interference graph over the readers, wherein there is an edge between two readers if they could lead to a reader-reader collision when transmitting simultaneously. 特别地，它考虑了读写器之间的干扰图，如果两个读写器在同时传输时可能导致读写器-读写器碰撞，则它们之间存在一条边。

  • It then tries to randomly color the readers such that each pair of interfering readers has different colors. 然后它尝试随机给读写器上色，使得每对干扰读写器具有不同的颜色。

(1) Activation Range

  • Energy absorption: objects in the surrounding space (metal, water, etc.) will absorb the wireless signal, thus distorting the signal transmission 能量吸收：周围空间的物体（如金属、水等）会吸收无线信号，从而扭曲信号传输

(2) External Environment—Identification Reliability

  • When an RFID reader performs a read operation in a noisy environment, it is possible for the reader to collect a report of a tag that is not in the read area, which is called Ghost reads. 当RFID读写器在嘈杂环境中执行读取操作时，可能会收集到不在读取区域内的标签报告，这被称为幽灵读取。

​

  • Gen 2 was specifically designed to address and minimize the occurrence of ghost reads: roughly 1 ghost read occurs per 1,000 tags. Gen 2专门设计用于解决和减少幽灵读取的发生：大约每1000个标签中有1个幽灵读取。

  • Gen 2 provides five sequential tests that serve as checks for a tag’s validity: Gen 2提供了五个顺序测试，用于检查标签的有效性：

  1. Tag response time: Tags must respond to a reader within a very short, defined time frame. If the tag response is not timed exactly from the start to the end of the response, the probability that the tag is a phantom is high. 标签响应时间： 标签必须在非常短的定义时间范围内响应读写器。如果标签响应的时间不是从开始到结束完全准确，那么标签是幽灵的概率很高。

  2. Preamble: For each and every response, tags first send a signal called a preamble. 前导码： 对于每个响应，标签首先发送一个称为前导码的信号。

  3. EPC format check: If the preamble is validated, the reader then examines the bit stream transmission to ensure it is in a valid EPC format. EPC格式检查： 如果前导码被验证，读写器会检查比特流传输以确保其为有效的EPC格式。

  4. Bit match: The reader compares the number of bits the Gen 2 tag reported that it would be sending to the number of bits received. 比特匹配： 读写器比较Gen 2标签报告的将要发送的比特数与接收到的比特数。

  5. CRC: The CRC checks for bit errors in transmission by comparing the number of bits the tag stated it would send with the number of bits actually sent. CRC： CRC通过比较标签声明的将要发送的比特数与实际发送的比特数来检查传输中的比特错误。

  • If any test is invalid, the reader discontinues communication and moves on to the next tag signal. If the considered test is valid, the reader checks the next test. 如果任何测试无效，读写器将停止通信并转到下一个标签信号。如果所考虑的测试有效，读写器将检查下一个测试。

(2) Effect of antenna radiation angle 天线辐射角度的影响

<1> Change the antenna angle with the unchanged tag position 固定标签，改变天线角度

  • Influence of the angle between radiation direction and the tag plane on the recognition performance 辐射方向与标签平面之间的夹角对识别性能的影响

  • For a specific tag, when the radiation angle is small (radiation direction and the tag close to the parallel state), the energy which reaches the tag is small. To activate the label, it is necessary to increase the reader’s transmit power. When the radiation angle is large (radiation direction and the label close to the orthogonal state), the required transmit power of reader is smaller. 对于一个特定标签，当辐射角度小时（辐射方向和标签接近平行状态），到达标签的能量较小，要激活标签，需要增加读写器的发射功率。当辐射角度较大时（辐射方向和标签接近正交状态），所需的读写器发射功率较小。

  • For a certain number of tags, the smaller the radiation angle of the antenna, the smaller the number of tags that can be recognized. When the radiation angle increases, the number of tags that can be recognized increases. 对于一定数量的标签，天线的辐射角度越小，可识别的标签数量越少。当辐射角度增加时，可识别的标签数量也增加。

<2> Change the tag angle with the unchanged antenna position 固定天线，改变标签角度

  • When the tag plane is vertical to the radiation direction of the antenna, the recognition performance is the best, and the transmitting power of the reader to recognize a tag is minimum. 当标签平面垂直于天线的辐射方向时，识别性能最佳，读写器识别标签所需的发射功率最小。

  • When the tag rotates around its own long axis(Z轴), the recognition performance is almost unaffected. 当标签绕其自身长轴旋转时，识别性能几乎不受影响。

  • When the tag rotates around its own short axis（Y轴）, the impact on the performance is the largest. 当标签绕其自身短轴旋转时，对性能的影响最大。

  • When the tag plane is approaching parallel to the antenna radiation direction, the identification performance is the worst. 当标签平面接近平行于天线的辐射方向时，识别性能最差。

总结：Improve recognition performance

  • Make the central radiation direction of antenna be perpendicular to the tag plane 使天线的中心辐射方向垂直于标签平面

  • Adjust the antenna to face the identified tag 调整天线，以面对识别的标签

(3) Effect of distance 距离的影响

  • In order to improve the tag recognition rate, we should decrease the distance between antenna and tag. At the same time, the recognition rate is difficult to reach 100%. We can also take some additional measures to improve the recognition rate, e.g, changing the position of the antenna (reducing the effect of multipath effects), reading the tag again, et al. 为了提高标签识别率，应降低天线与标签之间的距离。同时，识别率难以达到100%。我们也可以采取一些额外的措施来提高识别率，例如，改变天线的位置（减少多路径效应的影响），再次读取标签等。

(4) Effect of tag deployment density 标签部署密度的影响

  • 如图所示，能被成功识别的标签主要在中心区域，且部署的标签密度越大，阅读器的有效识别范围就越小，反之越大。
  • In realistic environments, the effective identification range of the reader is affected by the tag deployment density. We cannot use the theoretical antenna radiation model to calculate the effective identification area. If the tag deployment density is large, making the reader’s effective recognition area smaller, we can increase the reader’s transmitting power, or move the reader to scan multiple times, or do operations to read more tags and improve the system performance. 在现实环境中，读者的有效识别范围受到标签部署密度的影响。我们不能用理论的天线辐射模型来计算有效的识别面积。如果标签部署密度较大，使读卡器的有效识别区域较小，我们可以增加阅读器的传输功率，或者移动阅读器进行扫描多次，或者进行操作来读取更多的标签，提高系统性能。

(5) Effect of adjusting device location 调整设备位置的影响

  • If you want to identify tags in the current region as many as possible, you can reduce the impact of multipath effects by changing the relative position of the antenna and the tag, such as moving the position of the antenna, moving the tag, and so on. 如果你想在当前区域内尽可能多地识别标签，可以通过改变天线和标签的相对位置来减少多径效应的影响，比如移动天线的位置，移动标签等。

  • We can also reduce the impact of multipath effects by increasing the distance between the tag and the ground. 我们也可以通过增加标签与地面的距离来减少多径效应的影响。

  • The reader’s transmitting power, antenna radiation angle, and distance between the antenna and tag have an effect on the overall recognition time, all because they affect the number of identified tags. 读写器的发射功率、天线辐射角度和天线与标签之间的距离都会影响总体识别时间，因为它们影响识别的标签数量。

  • For each tag, its recognition time is independent of other factors such as the reader’s transmitting power, the antenna’s radiation angle, distance between the antenna and tag, and so on. 对于每个标签，其识别时间独立于其他因素，如读写器的发射功率、天线的辐射角度、天线与标签之间的距离等。

  • In a realistic environment, when we need to identify the same number of tags, we can use the appropriate method (adjusting the reader power, antenna radiation angle, and distance between the tag and antenna, etc.) as needed, without having to consider the effects of adjusting system parameters on identification time. 在实际环境中，当我们需要识别相同数量的标签时，可以根据需要使用适当的方法（调整读写器功率、天线辐射角度以及标签和天线之间的距离等），而无需考虑调整系统参数对识别时间的影响。

4. Problem and inspiration 问题与启发

(1) Difference Between Actual System and Theoretical Model 实际系统与理论模型的差异

  • In theory, the RF electromagnetic waves emitted by the RFID antenna diverge in a spherical or conical shape. The RF electromagnetic waves of an omnidirectional antenna spread around a ball to the surrounding, and a directional antenna spread around a conical shape with a certain direction. Thus, theoretically, the read range projected to a plane should be a regular circular area. The energy density of the center of the reader’s recognition area is the largest, and the energy density decreases as the circular area expands. 理论上，RFID天线发射的射频电磁波以球形或锥形发散。其中，全向天线的射频电磁波像球一样向四周扩散，而定向天线则以特定方向锥形扩散。因此，理论上投射到平面上的读取范围应该是一个规则的圆形区域。读写器识别区域中心的能量密度最大，能量密度随着圆形区域的扩展而减小。

  • In realistic cases, due to the path loss, multipath effect, signal interference and other factors, the effective recognition area of the reader is not a regular circular area. 在实际情况下，由于路径损耗、多径效应、信号干扰等因素，读写器的有效识别区域不是规则的圆形区域。

  • The tags in the reader’s identification area are not always readable. Due to factors such as multipath effect, the tag recognition rate is difficult to reach 100%. 读写器识别区域内的标签并非总是可读的。由于多径效应等因素，标签识别率难以达到100%。

(2) Strategies in Actual System 实际系统的策略

  1. Simplify the Algorithm: In the actual system implementation, for easy operation, we can appropriately simplify the algorithm under the condition of following the standard (e.g., EPC C1G2), making the system work more stably. 简化算法： 在实际系统实施中，为了操作方便，可以在遵循标准（如EPC C1G2）的条件下适当简化算法，使系统运行更稳定。

  2. Improve Tag Recognition Performance: In order to improve the tag recognition performance, it is necessary to make the antenna face the tag as much as possible so that the tag is in the central area of the reader’s recognition range. If you want to identify as many tags as possible, you can increase the reader’s transmitting power, improving the recognition performance. 提高标签识别性能： 为了提高标签识别性能，有必要尽量使天线面对标签，使标签处于读写器识别范围的中心区域。如果你想识别尽可能多的标签，可以增加读写器的发射功率，改善识别性能。

  3. Reduce Distance Between Antenna and Tag: When the position of the antenna or tag can be changed, try to reduce the distance between them in order to improve the tag recognition rate and reduce the number of missing reading tags. 减少天线和标签之间的距离： 当天线或标签的位置可以改变时，尽量减少它们之间的距离，以提高标签识别率并减少漏读标签的数量。

  4. Move Antenna or Tag Location: In order to ensure high recognition rate, we can move the antenna or tag location to reduce the impact of multipath effects and other factors, to reduce the tags in the blind identification area. 移动天线或标签位置： 为了确保高识别率，可以移动天线或标签位置，以减少多径效应和其他因素的影响，减少盲识别区域的标签。

  5. Consider Actual Environmental Factors: We need to consider the actual environmental factors (such as tag deployment density, multipath effects, etc.), effectively adjust the system parameters. We cannot blindly use the theoretical model to calculate the system identification performance. 考虑实际环境因素： 我们需要考虑实际的环境因素（如标签部署密度、多径效应等），有效地调整系统参数。不能盲目使用理论模型来计算系统识别性能。

We analyze the differences between the actual system and the theoretical model and propose some strategies for improving the performance of the RFID system in real environments. 随着RFID技术的不断发展，实际的RFID系统已经进入了人们的生活。为了提高RFID系统在实际环境中的性能，我们以Alien-9900+读写器、Alien-9611天线、Alien 9640标签为例，探索RFID系统在现实环境中的工作手段和性能。本文分析了实际系统与理论模型的差异，并提出了一些提高RFID系统在实际环境中性能的策略。
