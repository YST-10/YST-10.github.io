Lecture 10: Applications and Practice

1. Technical characteristics and advantages of RFID RFID的技术特点和优势

  • Core Theory 核心理论

    • Based on wireless communication theory and memory technology, RFID tags can be accessed by the reader as identifiers without needing line-of-sight communication. RFID技术的核心理论是基于无线通信理论和存储技术，RFID标签可以被阅读器作为标识符访问，而不需要视线通信。

  • save costs - reuse

  • Automatic Identification 自动识别

    • Can automatically collect information, the object is associated with the environment——IoT 可以自动收集信息，对象与环境关联——物联网（IoT）

(1) Non-contact, active, and rapid identification 非接触式的主动快速识别

Fast：1ms to complete the communication process entir 快速：1毫秒即可完成整个通信过程

Communication range：The relative position of the reader and the tag is not required. Effective identification range can be up to tens of meters. Many application scenarios 通信范围：不要求读写器和标签的相对位置。有效识别范围可达几十米。适用于多种应用场景

Active identification:The main feature of RFID technology is the ability to actively identify the tag, without specific operation. 主动识别：RFID技术的主要特点是能够主动识别标签，无需特定操作。

(2) Permanently store datay 永久存储数据

Data Capacity：2D barcode stores up to 2710 digits while the maximum capacity of RFID tags is up to several MB. 数据容量：二维条形码可存储多达2710个数字，而RFID标签的最大容量可达几MB。

Working Life：RFID surface without bare chip adapts to a variety of harsh environments, with a life of more than 10 years. 工作寿命：RFID表面无裸芯片，适应各种恶劣环境，寿命超过10年。

(3) Reusable 可重复使用

  • The RFID tag can store the information to be overwritten. RFID标签可以存储要覆盖的信息。

  • Rewriting time up to 100,000 times. 重写次数可达100,000次。

  • Change tag information to save costs. （换物品）只需更改标签信息以节省成本。

(4) Can be Read Simultaneously 可同时读取

  • The reader can simultaneously read and identify several RFID tags. 读写器可以同时读取和识别多个RFID标签。

  • Save time, and improve work efficiency. 节省时间，提高工作效率。

(5) Low Cost, Can be Deployed in Large-scale 成本低廉，可以大量部署

  • Relative to barcode technology, the cost is not dominant. 相对于条形码技术，成本没有优势。

  • Can get a higher price after large-scale production. 大规模生产后可以获得更高的价格。

  • RFID tags can be reused, cost-performance further improved. RFID标签可以重复使用，性价比进一步提高。

(6) Strong Penetration 具有很强的穿透性

  • Communicate through electromagnetic field. 通过电磁场进行通信。

  • Communicate when obstructed. 有障碍物时也能通信。

  • Passive tag: Effective recognition distance of about 10m. 无源标签：有效识别距离约10米。

  • Active label: Effective identification distance of up to 30m or more. 有源标签：有效识别距离可达30米或以上。

(7) Physical Characteristics of Traditional Wireless Technology 传统无线技术的物理特性

  • RFID has physical characteristics of general wireless technology. RFID具有一般无线技术的物理特性。

  • Signal strength and strong attenuation during transmission. 信号强度在传输过程中衰减严重。

(8) High Confidentiality 保密性较高

  • RFID tags are shipped with a unique number that cannot be changed. RFID标签附带唯一编号，不可更改。

  • Two-way authentication between RFID tags and readers. RFID标签和读写器之间的双向认证。

  • Tags and reader perform three mutual authentication before processing. 标签和读写器在处理前进行三次相互认证。

  • All data in the communication process is encrypted. 通信过程中的所有数据都经过加密。

  • Each sector in the tag has its own operating password and access conditions. 标签中的每个扇区都有自己的操作密码和访问条件。

Lecture 11: RFID Security and Sociocultural Implications RFID安全和社会文化影响

1. Market Trends and Usage 市场趋势和用途

(1) Market Trends

  • There is a large field of new and promising RFID applications. RFID有着广阔的新兴应用领域。
  • The most intensive and extensive use of RFID raises major issues in the areas of privacy, security, technological reliability, and international compatibility. RFID的广泛使用在隐私、安全、技术可靠性和国际兼容性方面引发了重大问题。
  • One key challenge for decision-makers is to create a common vision and a set of goals on how RFID can keep companies (and countries) more innovative and competitive in the world economy. 决策者面临的一个关键挑战是如何制定一个共同愿景和一套目标，使RFID帮助公司（和国家）在世界经济中保持创新和竞争力。
  • At present, technological challenges prevent wide adoption of RFID technology: 目前，技术挑战阻碍了RFID技术的广泛应用：
    • Lack of global frequency standards, low reading rates, interference with other radio sources, insufficient encryption capabilities, and the cost of implementation and end-user concerns. 缺乏全球频率标准、读取率低、与其他无线电源干扰、加密能力不足、实施成本和终端用户关切。
  • The main issues to be addressed are consumer privacy, standards and interoperability, harmonization of the frequency spectrum, intellectual property rights, and future research needs. 需要解决的主要问题是消费者隐私、标准和互操作性、频谱协调、知识产权和未来研究需求。

(2) Barriers to RFID Adoption RFID采用的障碍

  • Two principal barriers to global implementations for low-powered tags: the regulations and the laws of physics. 低功耗标签全球实施的两个主要障碍：法规和物理法则。

  • RFID systems can generally be grouped into two types: RFID系统通常分为两种类型：

    • Low-cost, but capable passive tag systems; 低成本但功能强大的无源标签系统；
    • High-cost specialized tags for operation at high speed and long distances. 高成本的专用标签，用于高速和长距离操作。

  • RFID supporters envision a world where RFID reader devices are everywhere: in stores, cars, clothes, factories, etc. RFID支持者设想了一个RFID读写设备无处不在的世界：在商店、汽车、衣服、工厂等。

  • However, RFID tags will not become ubiquitous in consumer products as their price is viewed as prohibitively expensive by many businesses. 然而，RFID标签不会在消费品中普及，因为许多企业认为其价格过于昂贵。

  • In the supply chain, the cost of tags is one of the main considerations for mass adoption, with the 5-cent tag being the much-discussed target. 在供应链中，标签的成本是大规模采用的主要考虑因素之一，目标是备受讨论的5美分标签。

  • IC companies have managed to get die sizes (chip area) down to around 0.3 mm² for UHF chips, resulting in ½ cent for manufacturing cost depending on the silicon process, leaving 3 cents for the rest of the cost. IC公司已经成功将UHF芯片的芯片面积缩小到约0.3平方毫米，制造成本约为0.5美分，剩余成本为3美分，具体取决于硅工艺。

  • These less expensive tags may lack the capabilities of their more expensive counterparts. Users of the tags are employing them in applications no one dreamed of a decade ago. 这些较便宜的标签可能缺乏其更昂贵的同类标签的功能。标签的用户正在将它们应用于十年前无人能想到的应用中。

2. RFID security and privacy aspects

(1) Security of RFID Systems

Application Context of a Typical RFID System Including Parties with Diverging Interests 一个典型的RFID系统的应用上下文，包括兴趣不同的各方

(2) Attacks on RFID Systems

<1> Attacks on the Transponder

  • Usually, the transponder is easily accessible. Therefore, there is a wide range of attacks with varying degrees of effectiveness. 通常，标签很容易接触到。因此，有各种攻击，效果各异。

  • Permanent destruction of the transponder 标签的永久破坏

    • Transponder shielding/tuning 标签屏蔽/调谐

    • Spoofing and cloning of transponders 标签的欺骗和克隆

<2> Attacks on the RF Interface

  • RFID systems are radio systems and communicate via electromagnetic waves in the near-field and the far-field range. RFID系统是无线电系统，通过近场和远场范围内的电磁波进行通信。
  • An attacker is therefore likely to try and attack an RFID system via the RF interface. Such an attack is attractive as it does not require any physical access to the reader or transponder but can be carried out from a distance. 因此，攻击者可能会尝试通过RF接口攻击RFID系统。这种攻击很有吸引力，因为它不需要对读写器或标签进行任何物理访问，但可以从远处进行。

Currently, the following attacks are known and have been investigated: 目前，已知并已研究了以下攻击：

I. Eavesdropping:

Eavesdropping: interception of the communication between reader and transponder 窃听：拦截阅读器和标签之间的通信(左图)

  • 阅读器通过下行链路（从读写器到标签）向应答器发送信号。
  • 应答器通过上行链路（从标签到读写器）向读写器发送响应信号。
  • 攻击者在窃听距离内放置一个接收器来截获这些信号。攻击者可以捕获下行链路和上行链路的通信，从而可能获得未经授权的信息。

II. Jamming:

Jamming: interruption of the communication between reader and transponder 干扰：中断读写器和标签之间的通信(右图)

  • 授权的交互：用户和RFID标签之间的正常通信过程，这里是合法的互动。
  • 被动监听：攻击者尝试通过被动方式监听用户和RFID标签之间的通信，但在这张图中主要说明了干扰攻击的部分。
  • 干扰（Jamming）：攻击者通过发送干扰信号来打断读写器和RFID标签之间的通信。干扰信号可以阻止用户与RFID标签之间的正常通信，导致信息传输失败。
  • 被动监听显示攻击者可能会尝试监听通信，但主要目的是通过干扰信号中断通信。

III. Extending the read range:

Extending the read range: to be able to skim a remote transponder, without being detected 扩展读取范围：能够在不被检测到的情况下扫描远程标签

  • Extending the read range of a reader might be an interesting option for an attacker. 扩展读写器的读取范围可能对攻击者来说是一个有趣的选择。
  • This way, the attacker may be able to read the transponder from a safe distance, without being detected. 这样，攻击者可以在安全距离内读取标签，而不会被检测到。
  • However, especially regarding the read range, technical opportunities and physical limits of RFID systems are often widely overestimated. 然而，特别是对于读取范围，RFID系统的技术机会和物理限制往往被广泛高估。
  • Due to the large difference between inductive coupling and backscatter process, we will discuss these two separately. 由于感应耦合和背散射过程之间的巨大差异，我们将分别讨论这两者。
  • Inductive Coupling 感应耦合

    • If a transponder is moved beyond the normal read range of such RFID systems, the communication can be disrupted for two different reasons. 如果标签移动到这种RFID系统的正常读取范围之外，通信可能会因两个不同的原因中断。

      1. One possible reason is that the transponder simply does not receive sufficient power from its antenna to be able to operate. 一个可能的原因是标签从天线接收的电力不足，无法操作。

      2. Another possible reason is that the transponder is supplied sufficient power to operate, but that the amplitude of the generated load modulation is no longer sufficiently large to be detected by the reader. 另一个可能的原因是标签获得了足够的电力来操作，但生成的负载调制幅度不再足够大，无法被读写器检测到。

    • The maximum reach of the power supply is called the energy range of the system; as opposed to the load modulation range, i.e. the maximum distance between transponder and reading antenna at which the reader still is able to detect the transponder’s load modulation. 电源的最大覆盖范围称为系统的能量范围；与负载调制范围相对，即标签和读取天线之间的最大距离，阅读器仍能检测到标签的负载调制。

  • Backscatter Coupling 后向散射耦合

    • If the transponder moves beyond the read range of the backscatter system, the communication can be disrupted for two different reasons. 如果标签移动到背散射系统的读取范围之外，通信可能会因两个不同的原因中断。

      • An obvious reason is an insufficient energy supply ( P_e ) of the transponder to be operated via the antenna. 一个明显的原因是通过天线操作标签的能量供应 ( P_e ) 不足。

      • However, it is just as likely that the transponder still has sufficient power to operate, but that the reflected power ( P_s ) is no longer sufficiently large to be detected by the reader. 然而，同样可能的是标签仍有足够的电力来操作，但反射功率 ( P_s ) 不再足够大，无法被读写器检测到。

    • For today’s backscatter systems, the energy intake of the transponder chip, i.e. energy ( P_e ) required for transponder operation, is decisive for the range of a system. 对于当今的背散射系统，标签芯片的能量摄入，即标签操作所需的能量 ( P_e )，对系统的范围起决定作用。

    • We call this range the energy range of the system; as opposed to the backscatter range which is the theoretical range of the signal reflected by the transponder antenna. 我们称这个范围为系统的能量范围；与背散射范围相对，即标签天线反射信号的理论范围。

    • An obvious option for increasing the range is an increased transmission power of the reader. 增加范围的一个明显选项是增加读写器的发射功率。

IV. Denial of Service (DOS) attacks:

Denial of Service (DOS) attacks: blocking a reader 拒绝服务（DOS）攻击：阻塞读写器

  • The blocker tag pretends to the attacked reader that there are ( 2^k ) transponders within its interrogation field where ( k ) is the number of bits in the serial number. Prompting such a large number of serial numbers literally blocks the affected reader. 阻塞标签通过伪装成大量应答器来欺骗受攻击的读写器，使其认为在其查询范围内有(2^k)个应答器（其中(k)是序列号中的位数）。这种大量的序列号会使得读写器的查询速度极其缓慢，甚至完全停止，导致系统被阻塞。
  • The blocker tag spoofs the tree-walking protocol into thinking that all tags, that is, all identifiers, are present. To do this, it simply emits both a 0 and a 1 in response to all reader queries. 阻塞标签通过欺骗树行走协议，使其认为所有标签（即所有标识符）都存在。为了做到这一点，阻塞标签对所有读写器的查询同时发出0和1的响应。

    • 时间帧中，“0”表示空槽，“1”表示单标签槽，“C”表示碰撞槽。
    • 由于阻塞标签的存在，造成了多次碰撞，使得读写器无法正确读取实际存在的标签。

  • 右图 (b)：展示了树行走协议的欺骗过程。

    • 图中显示了读写器尝试通过树行走协议来识别标签的过程。

V. Relay Attack:

Relay Attack: undetected use of a remote transponder 中继攻击：未被检测到的远程标签使用

  • A relay attack pretends to a reader that a remote transponder lies within the reader’s read range. This way, the attacker can prompt actions that otherwise require the physical presence of the transponder close to the reader (e.g., access systems, payment systems, etc). 中继攻击向读卡器假装远程应答器位于读卡器的读取范围内。通过这种方式，攻击者可以提示操作，否则需要应答器靠近阅读器的物理存在（例如，访问系统、支付系统等）

  • 读写器（Reader）：发送和接收信号以与RFID标签通信的设备。

  • 幽灵代理（Ghost, Proxy）：假装为实际标签的设备，在读写器的读取范围内。

    • 该设备位于读写器的读取范围内，接收从吸血鬼设备传来的信号。
    • 将这些信号传递给读写器，使读写器误以为应答器在其读取范围内。

  • 吸血鬼设备（Leech, Mole）：与实际标签通信的设备，通过远距离连接将标签信息传递给幽灵代理。

    • 该设备靠近实际的应答器，与应答器通信，并捕获其信号。
    • 通过远距离连接将这些信号传送给幽灵代理。

  • 应答器（Transponder）：实际的RFID标签。

  • 远距离连接：连接幽灵代理和吸血鬼设备的通信链路，使得它们能够在长距离内协同工作。

  • M点：幽灵代理和吸血鬼设备各自与读写器和应答器的交互点。

  • 通过这种方式，攻击者可以让读写器认为远距离的应答器在其读取范围内，从而诱发需要应答器实际存在的操作（例如访问控制系统、支付系统等）。

(3) 保护方式

<1> Protection by Cryptographic Measures 通过密码措施的保护

  • High-security RFID systems must have a defense against the following individual attacks: 高安全性RFID系统必须防御以下单个攻击：

    • Skimming of a data carrier in order to clone and/or modify data; 读取数据载体以克隆和/或修改数据；

    • Placing a foreign data carrier within the interrogation zone of a reader with the intention of gaining unauthorized access to a building or receiving services without payment; 在读写器的询问区域内放置外部数据载体，意图未经授权进入建筑物或无支付接收服务；

    • Eavesdropping on radio communications and replaying the data, to imitate a genuine data carrier (‘replay and fraud’). 窃听无线电通信并重放数据，以模仿真实的数据载体（“重放和欺诈”）。

  • When selecting a suitable RFID system, consideration should be given to cryptological functions. 在选择合适的RFID系统时，应考虑密码学功能。

    • Applications that do not require a security function (e.g., industrial automation, tool recognition) would be made unnecessarily expensive by the incorporation of cryptological procedures. 不需要安全功能的应用（如工业自动化、工具识别）因引入密码学程序而变得不必要地昂贵。
    • In high-security applications (e.g., ticketing, payment systems) the omission of cryptological procedures can be a very expensive oversight if manipulated transponders are used to gain access to services without authorization. 在高安全性应用（如售票、支付系统）中，如果省略密码学程序，使用操纵标签未经授权获取服务可能会造成非常昂贵的疏漏。

<2> Mutual Symmetrical Authentication 相互对称认证

  1. 获取挑战（GET_CHALLENGE）：读写器向应答器发送一个认证请求。
  2. 应答器生成随机数（Random A）：应答器生成一个随机数A，并将其发送给读写器。
    • 确保每次认证过程都是唯一的，防止重放攻击。
  3. 令牌1（Token 1）：
    • 读写器使用共享密钥K和接收到的随机数A来生成令牌1，并将其发送给应答器。
  4. 令牌2（Token 2）：
    • 应答器接收到令牌1后，验证其正确性。
    • 验证通过后，应答器使用共享密钥K和随机数A来生成令牌2，并将其发送回读写器。

<3> Authentication Using Derived Keys 使用派生的密钥进行身份验证

  • In an authentication procedure based upon derived keys, a key unique to the transponder is first calculated in the reader from the serial number (ID number) of the transponder. This key must then be used for authentication. 在基于派生密钥的认证过程中，首先在读写器中根据标签的序列号（ID号）计算出唯一的密钥。然后必须使用此密钥进行认证。

  1. 获取ID（GET_ID）： 读写器向应答器请求其ID号。

  2. 应答器提供ID号（ID-Number）：应答器将其唯一的ID号发送给读写器。

  3. 计算派生密钥：

    • 读写器使用主密钥( K_M )和从应答器接收到的ID号，计算出一个唯一的派生密钥( K_{X} )。
    • 这个过程在生产时已经在应答器中完成，因此应答器也拥有相同的派生密钥( K_{X} )。

  4. 获取挑战（GET_CHALLENGE）：

    • 读写器向应答器发送一个认证请求，并生成一个随机数A。

  5. 令牌1（Token 1）：

    • 应答器使用派生密钥( K_{X} )和随机数A生成令牌1，并将其发送给读写器。

  6. 令牌2（Token 2）：

    • 读写器接收到令牌1后，验证其正确性。
    • 验证通过后，读写器使用派生密钥( K_{X} )和随机数A生成令牌2，并将其发送回应答器。

<4> Encrypted Data Transfer 数据传输加密

  • We can differentiate between two basic types of attack: 我们可以区分两种基本类型的攻击：

  • Passive Attacker: Attacker 1 behaves passively and tries to eavesdrop on the transmission to discover confidential information for wrongful purposes. 被动攻击者： 攻击者1的行为是被动的，并试图窃听传输，以发现机密信息。

    • Active Attacker: Attacker 2, on the other hand, behaves actively to manipulate the transmitted data and alter it to his benefit 主动攻击者： 另一方面，攻击者2积极地操纵传输的数据并将其修改以造福自己。

  • The cipher data is transformed back to its original form in the receiver using the secret key K and the secret algorithm (→ decryption, deciphering). 加密数据在接收端使用密钥K和加密算法转换回其原始形式（→解密）。

  • Cryptological procedures are used to protect against both passive and active attacks. 密码学程序用于防护被动和主动攻击。

  • To achieve this, the transmitted data (plain text) can be altered (encrypted) prior to transmission so that a potential attacker can no longer draw conclusions about the actual content of the message (plain text). 为此，传输前可以对传输的数据（明文）进行更改（加密），使潜在攻击者无法再推断消息的实际内容（明文）。

  • Encrypted data transmission always takes place according to the same pattern. The transmission data (plain text) is transformed into cipher data (cipher text) (→ encryption, ciphering) using a secret key K and a cryptographical algorithm. 加密数据传输总是按照相同的模式进行。使用密钥K和加密算法将传输数据（明文）转换为加密数据（密文）（→加密）。

  • Without knowing the encryption algorithm and the secret key K, a potential attacker is unable to interpret the recorded data. It is not possible to recreate the transmission data from the cipher data. 在不知道加密算法和密钥K的情况下，潜在攻击者无法解释记录的数据。不可能从加密数据中重新创建传输数据。

  • If the keys K for ciphering and K for deciphering are identical (K = K) or in a direct relationship to each other, the procedure is a symmetrical key procedure. 如果用于加密和解密的密钥K相同（K = K）或彼此直接相关，则该程序为对称密钥程序。

  • If knowledge of the key K is irrelevant to the deciphering process, the procedure is an asymmetrical key procedure. 如果密钥K的知识与解密过程无关，则该程序为非对称密钥程序。

  • RFID systems have for a long time used only symmetrical procedures, therefore we will not describe other procedures in further detail here. RFID系统长期以来只使用对称程序，因此我们不会在此详细描述其他程序。

  • If each character is individually encrypted prior to transmission, the procedure is known as sequential ciphering (or stream ciphering). If, on the other hand, several characters are incorporated into a block, then we talk of a block cipher. Because block ciphers are generally very calculation intensive, they play a less important role in RFID systems. Therefore the emphasis is placed on sequential ciphers in what follows. 如果在传输前对每个字符单独加密，则该程序称为序列加密（或流加密）。另一方面，如果将几个字符合并到一个块中，则我们称之为块加密。由于块加密通常计算量很大，它们在RFID系统中起的作用较小。因此，以下重点放在序列加密上。

<5> Stream Cipher 流加密

  • The principle underlying the generation of a secure key by a pseudorandom generator. 通过伪随机生成器生成安全密钥的原理。

  • Basic circuit of a pseudorandom generator incorporating a linear feedback shift register (LFSR) 包含线性反馈移位寄存器（LFSR）的伪随机发生器的基本电路

  1. 密钥生成：

    • 秘密密钥 ( K ) 输入到伪随机数生成器中。
    • 通过函数 ( g(K) )，生成一个伪随机数序列 ( M )。

  2. 伪随机数生成：

    • 使用 ( g(K) ) 函数对密钥 ( K ) 进行处理，生成一个伪随机数序列。
    • 这个序列是由移位寄存器和计时脉冲控制的，确保其随机性。

  3. 加密过程：

    • 将传输数据和伪随机数序列进行逐位（bitwise）的异或（XOR）操作。
    • 通过函数 ( f(K) )，对传输数据的每一位和生成的伪随机数进行异或运算，生成密文数据（Cipher Data）。

<6> Kill Function

  • The EPCglobal standard specifies that tags must be equipped with at least one nullification function to address public opposition. EPCglobal标准规定，标签必须至少配备一个作废功能以应对公众反对意见。

  • This kill command disables the functionality of the tag after consumers purchase a product. 此“杀死”命令在消费者购买产品后禁用标签的功能。

    • It involves a high degree of consumer privacy protection at negligible cost. 它以可忽略的成本提供高度的消费者隐私保护。

    • However, since the disabling process is performed manually by millions of individual consumers, human error is always a possibility. 然而，由于禁用过程是由数百万个体消费者手动执行的，人为错误总是有可能发生。

  • Moreover, the major problem in killing the tag is that the various RFID stakeholders would no longer be able to take advantage of the future emerging services that would rely on the millions of RFID tags likely to be dispersed throughout the consumer environment. 此外，“杀死”标签的主要问题是，各种RFID利益相关者将无法利用未来新兴的服务，这些服务可能依赖于分散在消费者环境中的数百万个RFID标签。

  • This simple countermeasure, a built-in option designed to kill the functionality of an RFID tag when the consumer leaves the store, has been incorporated into the EPCglobal standard (Class 1 Generation 2 UHF Air Interface Protocol). 这种简单的对策，即设计为在消费者离开商店时杀死RFID标签功能的内置选项，已被纳入EPCglobal标准（第1类第2代UHF空中接口协议）。

  • However, killing a tag’s functionality curtails the future potential use of RFID in consumer services, such as in smart refrigerators that automatically reorder food products, expiration date and product recall alarms, and personal library management. 然而，杀死标签的功能会限制未来RFID在消费者服务中的潜在用途，例如自动重新订购食品的智能冰箱、到期日期和产品召回警报以及个人图书馆管理。

3. Health Risks from RFID

  • The report by The International Commission on Non-Ionizing Radiation Protection (ICNIRP) examines the effects of EM radiation on humans. The report describes mechanisms of thermal and nonthermal interaction between EM fields and biological systems. 国际非电离辐射防护委员会（ICNIRP）的报告研究了电磁辐射对人类的影响。报告描述了电磁场与生物系统之间的热和非热相互作用机制。

  • Thermal interaction is the heating of tissue which can cause damage. The most notable nonthermal interaction is brain stimulation, which means that the membrane potentials may be altered at a cellular level and might have effects on the nervous system. The report states that the high frequencies of EAS/RFID system produce no heating or thermoregulatory stress. 热相互作用 是组织加热，会造成损害。最显著的非热相互作用是脑刺激，这意味着膜电位可能在细胞水平上改变，并可能对神经系统产生影响。报告指出，EAS/RFID系统的高频率不会产生加热或体温调节压力。

  • However, EAS and RFID devices may interact with medical devices such as pacemakers, which can cause dangerous situations and indirect health hazards. 然而，EAS和RFID设备可能与心脏起搏器等医疗设备相互作用，可能导致危险情况和间接健康危害。

  • Since 1993, there has existed an electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standard for medical devices (MD), the IEC 60601-1-2. This standard was specified for frequencies up to 2.5 GHz. In the standard, it was specified that life-supporting equipment must be able to operate in the presence of field strengths up to 10 V/m and non-life-supporting up to 3 V/m. 自1993年以来，已有一个针对医疗设备（MD）的电磁兼容性（EMC）标准，即IEC 60601-1-2。该标准规定了最高达2.5 GHz的频率。在标准中，规定了生命支持设备必须能够在场强高达10 V/m的情况下工作，而非生命支持设备则高达3 V/m。

  • RFID transmitters and short-range radio devices are regulated differently in Europe and USA. RFID发射器和短距离无线设备在欧洲和美国的监管不同。

  • In Europe, regulations are country-specific based on the CEPT regulations. 在欧洲，法规是基于CEPT法规的国家特定法规。

    • In the USA, radio devices have to conform to the FCC. 在美国，无线设备必须符合FCC规定。

  • Some general types of recommendations are listed here: 一些一般性建议如下：

  • Continue to monitor current research on the health effects of exposure to radio frequencies from RFID equipment to verify whether new trends pose health risks. 继续监测当前关于RFID设备射频暴露对健康影响的研究，以验证新趋势是否构成健康风险。

    • Ensure that any RFID equipment purchased meets the FCC requirements. 确保购买的任何RFID设备符合FCC要求。

    • Purchase RFID equipment that meets the human exposure specifications of non-regulatory agencies such as the ANSI, the IEEE, the NCRP, American Conference of Industrial Hygienists (ACGIH), and the ICNIRP. 购买符合非监管机构（如ANSI、IEEE、NCRP、美国工业卫生学会（ACGIH）和ICNIRP）的人体暴露规范的RFID设备。

  • Conduct measurements of the RF emissions levels generated from RFID systems to ensure that the emissions are below those recommended under the OSHA non-ionizing standard (CFR 29, Section 1910.97) and by the non-regulatory agencies listed in the previous point. 测量RFID系统产生的RF辐射水平，以确保辐射低于OSHA非电离标准（CFR 29，1910.97节）和上文列出的非监管机构推荐的水平。

    • Ensure that RFID equipment is installed and maintained according to the manufacturers’ specifications. 确保RFID设备按照制造商的规格安装和维护。
